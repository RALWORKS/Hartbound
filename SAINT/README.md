# Hartbound: Age of Dusk

RALWORKS 2026

YOU are an Elf of the Age of Dusk, living only a decade before the Time of the Saint.

You are HARTBOUND - connected in Spirit to an elk, that keeps your soul from bleeding away from the mortal plain due to your Dreaming Sickness, contracted from your mother at birth.

Just as you are establishing yourself as an ELVEN GUIDE, you get swept up in an effort to COORDINATE THE LOCAL MILITIAS to FIGHT OFF THE RAIDERS OF THE CONQUERER KING seeking to take over the Elven and Human Kingdoms.

Together with your small party, you must spread the word of the rebellion. As MESSENGERS, you are the very thread that ties the movement together.

Your efforts will make history.
