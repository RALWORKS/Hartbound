# Saint of Mazes; Age of Dusk

RALWORKS 2023

After barely surviving a Demon attack, you find yourself terminally cursed, but also showing signs of being the Saint who can bring about the next Age. You must journey to the Temple of the Moon if you are to have any hope of survival past the year, and to face the challenges, if you dare, that will prove whether you are the prophecized Saint.

After getting separated from the priest guiding you, you must cross two more regions on your own--and both are deep in civil war. There are people willing to help you, if you help them--an ageing farmer, and later, the elves of an eastern village--but they are facing dangers to rival your own.

Journey across the land. Face down Demons. Reach the Temple--and maybe find, along the way, that you have a power that can bring a new Age--if the curse doesn't kill you before you can rise to wield it.
